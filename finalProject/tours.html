Snorkeling in the coral reef

World famous for its white sand beaches, Cozumel is also known for having beautiful underwater landscapes. Whether snorkeling or scuba diving, these are some of the mandatory activities in your trip. You will be guided by experts to the most beautiful places, even if this is the first time in your life.

Iguanas tour

Just by walking around the island you will encounter many iguanas lying on the streets, the beach, and palms. With us, you will also have the opportunity to feed one of of our little friends and have it standing on your head. So, don't go home without taking a picture to show your friends.

Releasing baby turtles

One of the most enriching experiences you'll ever see. After hatching from their eggs, we gather once a year at the end of July to release baby turtles into the sea. This activity increases the chance of survival for more than a thousand turtles. So, raise your hand if you're ready to participate!
