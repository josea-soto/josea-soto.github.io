Purpose

The site is connected to information from thousands of cities and can show historical and future weather data, hourly and weekly forecasts; including wind, pressure, humidity, and air quality. The purpose of the website is to help people get prepared for the weather conditions in their region, so they never forget their umbrella at home. The site is easy to navigate and allows the user to get the information they need in a matter of seconds.
